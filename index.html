Essentials

The Essentials Digital Construction Strategy focuses on core tools and processes to streamline project delivery, ensuring collaboration and reducing risks without unnecessary complexity.

Key benefits include:

  • Clear Communication: Use 3D models for site briefings and visual presentations, ensuring teams understand tasks and design intent.
  • Basic Site Efficiency: Digital forms and snagging processes linked to the model simplify on-site tasks and improve issue tracking.
  • Design Coordination: Shared, clash-free models align all disciplines, reducing errors and costly rework.
  • Integrated Visualisation: Tools like augmented reality provide real-time comparisons of design versus construction, enhancing quality control.

Smart

The Smart Digital Construction Strategy is designed to enhance project delivery by integrating advanced digital tools that reduce risks, improve efficiency, and streamline processes.

Key benefits include:

  • Survey Accuracy: Detailed 3D models of existing conditions and precise site surveys ensure early identification of risks, reducing costly design errors.
  • Design Precision: Enhanced design reviews and interdisciplinary checks ensure all disciplines are fully aligned, minimising clashes and rework.
  • Data Management: Consistent naming and embedded quantities improve traceability, ensuring accurate cost tracking and resource allocation.

Advanced

The Advanced Digital Construction Strategy takes digital construction to the next level, integrating advanced tools to reduce risks, boost efficiency, and streamline project delivery.

Key benefits include:

  • Accurate Planning: Advanced simulations and progress tracking ensure construction sequences are optimised, reducing delays and on-site disruptions.
  • Quality Assurance: Automated inspection plans and seamless handover documents improve accountability and ensure all project standards are met.
  • Cost and Resource Efficiency: Integrated costing tools and tender coding enable precise budget control and smarter resource allocation.
  • Sustainability Focus: Carbon footprint, lighting, and thermal performance analysis help meet environmental goals and reduce operational costs.
  • Comprehensive Insights: Structural and energy simulations provide a complete understanding of project performance, improving decision-making.
